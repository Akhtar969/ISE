Engineered for Performance

Our Range Of Piping Solutions

I.S.ENGINEERING CO. Pvt. Ltd. offers a comprehensive range of piping components designed to facilitate fluid flow management. Our product line includes items that redirect flow direction, such as elbows and tees, as well as those that modify pipe sizes, such as reducers and reducing tees. Additionally, we provide connectors like couplings for joining different components seamlessly, along with caps to halt flow when necessary. Our selection of pipe fittings encompasses a variety of components tailored to connect pipe ends in various configurations, including in-line, offset, multi-port, and mounting setups.

Applications:
• Plumbing and piping systems
• HVAC installations
• Chemical processing plants
• Oil and gas refineries
• Water treatment facilities
• Pharmaceutical manufacturing
• Food and beverage processing

Category:
Adapter, Coupling, Elbow, Flange, Nipple.

Benefits:
• Ensures reliable and leak-free connections.
• Facilitates efficient fluid flow and distribution.
• Reduces installation time and labor costs.
• Enhances system integrity and longevity.
• Compliant with industry standards and regulations.

Connection:
Threaded, Weld, Flange, Clamp End, End Bell.

Tee & Reducing Tee

Our Tee Fitting and Reducing Tee Fitting are indispensable components in fluid conveyance systems, adept at creating branch connections and accommodating changes in pipe diameter with precision and reliability. Engineered with meticulous detail and forged from high-grade materials, these fittings ensure seamless fluid distribution in piping networks across diverse industries.

Key Features

Versatile Junction: The Tee Fitting establishes a T-shaped intersection within piping setups, facilitating fluid diversion into two distinct branches, Conversely, the Reducing Tee Fitting incorporates one or more smaller diameter outlets, allowing for a gradual reduction in pipe size while maintaining flow integrity.


Rugged Build: Crafted from resilient materials, our Tee and Reducing Tee Fittings exhibit exceptional durability and corrosion resistance. guaranteeing enduring performance in rigorous industrial environments.


Smooth Flow Transition: Engineered with precision, our fittings boast smooth internal transitions that minimize flow resistance and pressure drop, optimizing fluid conveyance efficiency while upholding system functionality.
